★ CHARACTER PROFILE

Meet the chimera diva

Height 159.5 cm
Weight 47 kg
Age 31
Type Chimera

🧬 Origins

Born on April 1, 2008 as an April Fools' Day prank on 2channel. Users created a fake "VOCALOID" character with randomly selected traits. She was later turned into a real UTAU voicebank, becoming a "diva born from a hoax."

🎤 Voice

Voiced by Mayo Oyamano (小山乃 舞世). Available for UTAU, Synthesizer V AI, VOICEPEAK, and TALQu. Her voice is described as bright, energetic, and versatile.

🥖 Personality

Tsundere personality. Cheerful, eager, and a bit mischievous. Loves French bread (baguettes), playing tricks, and getting attention. Hates rats and people who mistake her drills for pigtails. Her favorite country is Norway!

💇 Appearance

Red eyes, red hair styled in iconic twin drills. Typically wears a dark blue/gray outfit with red trim. As a chimera, she's sometimes depicted with wings and a forked tail.

🎭 Fun Facts

  • Her name "Kasane" (重音) means "heavy sound" or "overlapped sound"
  • She's one of "The 3 UTAU Girls" alongside Uta Utane and Momo Momone
  • Her character code is 0401 (April 1st)
  • She can extend DVD rental periods with tricks
  • Her measurements: 73cm (bust), 54cm (waist), 88cm (hips)
